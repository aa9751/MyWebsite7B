Wicked

Wicked is a very well known musical performed worldwide. It first opened on Broadway on October 30th 2003. Idina Menzel (who also voiced Elsa in Frozen) played Elphaba and Kristin Chenoweth played Glinda when it opened. Since then Wicked was a huge hit and is now performed in places like the West End, Sydney. Broadway etc. Millions over the world pay to watch this musical.

Wicked was orignally a book written by Gregory Maguire - an American novelist. The book was published in 1995 and was later on turned into a musical.
